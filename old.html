LocalStorage Pads

These notepads will automatically save your notes in your browser's storage, persisting them through closing and restarts. Clearing your browser cache is not advised.

The different pads below do not share information storage - what you write in one won't effect the other.

Rich text is like Microsoft Word or other text editors, while Markdown is a syntax popularized by places like Github, Reddit, and Stackexchange.

To use, drag the bookmarklet links below onto your bookmarks bar. You can rename it.
